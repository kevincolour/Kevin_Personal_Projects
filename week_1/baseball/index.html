HOW TO PLAY

The Objective of the game is to guess the 4 digit, randomly generated number (the ANSWER) by guessing repeatedly.

There are couple of rules to this game to help you achieve the Objective.

  • The random genrated number(the ANSWER) does not have repeat digits. IE 0033 is NOT a valid number. 0123 is valid. 2301 is valid.
  • You must guess only 4 digits at a time.
  • If one of the digits in the number you provide matches the ANSWER, then a B is shown. If the ANSWER is 1234 and you guess 4321, there will be 4 "B"s.
  • If one of the digits in the number you provide is in the same column as the ANSWER, then a S is shown. If the ANSWER is 1234 and you guess 1243, there will be an output of "SSBB".

Good luck, and have fun. I used to play this game with my family when on vacations. We averaged around 10 guesses to find the answer.
